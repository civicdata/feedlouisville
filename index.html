Projects, Ideas and Planning

Miles for Merry Miracles

In 2017, three youth leaders started a new project called Good to Grow Green after a food drive that would benefit Angel tree kids netted 3466 packs of raman noodles in addition to 5000 other food items. G2GG focuses on educating youth about food choices, nutrition, gardening and philanthropy. The end goal to teach kids to eat well so they can live, learn, give and earn in their community is being realized. The project has received national attention and was recognized as a top 10 finisher at AllState Foundation's Good Starts Young Rally in Chicago. The group has plans to install 12 indoor classroom gardens in addition to the 4 they have already installed in the next 6 month.
